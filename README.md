### **1.1 Project Overview**
Abdul Bari‚Äôs Kindness Quest is a 3D educational adventure game designed to teach children Islamic values through interactive gameplay. Players help Abdul Bari perform good deeds such as helping others, caring for animals, and learning Islamic lessons.

---

### **1.2 Game Objectives**
- Teach children **Islamic moral values** such as honesty, compassion, and respect.
- Encourage acts of kindness, generosity, and community service.
- Strengthen children's understanding of **Islamic teachings** through simple actions and rewards.
- Provide an educational yet fun experience for Muslim families.

---

### **1.3 Why It Will Succeed**
- **Unique Focus**: Few games teach Islamic values, filling a significant niche.
- **Recognizable Character**: Abdul Bari‚Äôs established popularity ensures quick engagement.
- **Positive Impact**: Parents and educators will appreciate a game that teaches moral values.
- **Replayability**: New missions, unlockable areas, and achievements keep players motivated.

---

### **1.4 Story & Setting**
Abdul Bari lives in a peaceful Muslim neighborhood. Each day, he helps his family, community, and environment through small acts of kindness. His missions are inspired by Hadith and Quranic messages, reinforcing Islamic teachings as players progress through different environments such as home, park, mosque, and market.

---

### **Part 2: Gameplay Design**

**2.1 Core Gameplay Loop**
- Explore various areas (home, park, mosque, etc.)
- Perform good deeds (e.g., helping others, cleaning, feeding animals)
- Earn rewards (Kindness Coins) and unlock lessons (Hadith/Quranic quotes)
- Progress to the next mission and area.

**2.2 Gameplay Features**
- **Acts of Kindness:** Perform various good deeds like helping elders, cleaning, and donating.
- **Moral Lessons:** Earn Islamic quotes after every completed mission.
- **Kindness Coins:** In-game currency used for unlocking rewards and levels.
- **Badges & Titles:** Achievements like ‚ÄúHelper‚Äù or ‚ÄúKind Soul.‚Äù
- **Unlockable Areas:** New locations (school, mosque, park) open up as you progress.

**2.3 Controls and User Interface**
- **Mobile**: Swipe to move, tap to interact, and menu buttons.
- **PC**: Arrow keys to move, mouse click to interact.

---

### **Part 3: Art and Visual Design**
- **Art Style**: Cartoonish, 2D vector with smooth animation.
- **Character Design**: Abdul Bari and other characters designed in a friendly, engaging manner.
- **Environment Design**: Cozy home, clean park, serene mosque, and lively market.

---

### **Part 4: Learning & Education System**
- **Learning Through Play**: Acts of kindness lead to pop-up messages of Hadith and Quranic verses.
- **Quiz Mode**: Test players' knowledge of Islamic values after completing levels.
- **Moral Feedback**: Positive sound and visuals for good deeds, gentle encouragement for mistakes.
